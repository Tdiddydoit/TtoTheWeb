Understanding information architechture

What is information architechture

IA is the practice of organizing, structuring, and labeling content in an effected and sustainiable way. The goal is for users to find information and complete tasks. It is an important part of the user experience (UX) design.

The importance of IA

Good IA helps the user understand where they are, what they have found , what is around and what to expect. It helps with assesabilty, leads to better user satisfacton, reduced fustration, and they more easily find information
